TIGRENS HISTORY

2012 2013 2015 2016 2017 2019 2021 Established by Mr. Neo Nguyen, we embarked on our journey as a Magento extension provider under the name of AZEBIZ (E-business from A to Z).
 Underwent a rebranding from AZEBIZ to TIGREN, affirming our identity and expanding our focus to cater to a broader clientele.
Provided a comprehensive range of e-commerce services, from web and app development, website optimization, theme and extension development, to support and maintenance.
 Introduced the industry's first Progressive Web App extension, positioning ourselves as pioneers in providing PWA integration solutions for Magento websites.
 Expanded our service offerings to encompass Outsourcing website and app development.
 Recognized the potential of Magento 2 and initiated our Magento 2 Migration service. Commenced offering Progressive Web App development service.
Launched TigrenPWA theme
a ready-made solution to convert
any Magento 2 website to a PWA.

DISCOVER WHAT SETS US APART

Small but Mighty, Serving the Giants

Though small in size, our team possesses an unwavering dedication to turning "big dreams" into realities. With approximately 50 talented professionals, we bring passion, creativity, and technical expertise to every project we undertake.

Our clients encompass a wide spectrum, ranging from ambitious young entrepreneurs seeking to establish a simple online store today, with aspirations of becoming the next e-commerce powerhouse tomorrow, to mid-sized companies in search of cost-effective solutions to realize their grand visions.

Certifications may Impress, but Experience Speaks Louder

At Tigren, we value practical expertise over a collection of certificates. While others may chase accolades through theoretical exams, we have chosen a different path.

Our expertise is honed through a multitude of projects, ranging from small-scale ventures to large-scale enterprises. We let our tangible results and glowing client testimonials validate our capabilities.

Quality over Quantity - Satisfaction Above All

We take immense pride in the satisfaction of each and every client we serve. While we may not boast thousands of clients, we can confidently assert that all of our clients are unequivocally satisfied with our exceptional project execution and completion.
